## Executive Summary

Developed an interactive sales analytics dashboard to analyze revenue performance, profitability, and regional trends across 10,000+ simulated transactions.

The project demonstrates a complete analytics workflow including:

* Data generation
* Data cleaning
* SQL analysis
* Exploratory Data Analysis
* Dashboard development
* Business recommendations

---

## Business Problem

Organizations often struggle to quickly identify:

* Top-performing products
* Revenue growth trends
* Regional performance differences
* Profitability drivers

This dashboard provides business stakeholders with actionable insights through interactive visualizations and KPI monitoring.

---

## Key Results

📈 Revenue Growth Analysis Across 24 Months

💰 Profitability Tracking by Product Category

🌎 Regional Sales Performance Comparison

⚡ Reduced reporting effort through dashboard automation
